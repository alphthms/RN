2. Intial Ideas





Our final design is to use a crane that will be able to reach the bottom of the water. It will be attached to the top of the boat, allowing us to go to the bottom of the tank and pick up the objects to put onto the boat. The crane will be positioned so that the weight is dispersed throughout the boat, as well as allowing a lot of storage for the objects to be placed. Also, because of the many needed materials, the crane will rotate so that the mechanism is less likely to fail. It will have outriggers made of plastic attached to both sides of the boat.
